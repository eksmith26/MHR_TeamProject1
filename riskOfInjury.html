The investigation into falsified injury reports

In 2020, we updated our website to include the measures we’ve taken to increase safety for workers on the floor. Here we cite the statistics on the standard injury count for the industry in comparison to our factories based in the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. This was a good way for the company to prove that, while it’s not ok, Tesla reports less injuries per year on average than all other automotive industry competitors. We are going to choose these numbers as correct because there has been no formal trial proving falsification. However, investigative journalism site Reveal is claiming that Tesla falsified these reports based on anecdotal evidence from employees
